Sangeet Acharya

Ravjibhai Patel


Acharya shri Ravjibhai Patel is considered as one of the pioneers of classical music in Gujarat. He was a disciple of Pandit Narayan Moreshwar Khare.

Apart from a great artist of Kirana Gharana,he played a very important role of teaching and spreading classical music in Gujarat.



He traveled throughout Gujarat and united music teachers as well as music lovers.



Continuously for 15 years, he gave lessons on classical music on All India radio.



Ravji bhai successfully coordinated the activities of Gandharva Mahavidyalaya for 58 years, and also founded Bruhad Gujarat Sangeet Samiti (B.G.S.S.)

the examination board for conducting music exams in Gujarat in 1957.



Government of Gujarat awarded him with ‘Gaurav Puraskar’ for his contribution to the field of classical music.
